The SEO Science: Why "Alternative" Pages are SaaS Goldmines

In the SEO industry, targeting competitor brand names + "alternative" or "vs" is known as Comparison SEO or Conquesting.

The Claim: Comparison pages have the highest conversion rates of any organic page type.

The Official Backing: Industry leaders like Ahrefs and CXL Institute explicitly teach this. A user searching for "What is AI photo restoration" has Informational Intent (they are just learning). A user searching for "Remini Alternative" has Commercial Investigation / Transactional Intent. They have already experienced the problem, tried the market leader, found a flaw (too expensive, bad quality, creepy AI artifacts), and have their credit card in hand looking for a replacement.

The Strategy: You do not build these pages to aggressively bash the competitor. You build them to objectively compare features, actively acknowledging what the competitor does well, but heavily highlighting your Unique Selling Propositions (USPs) where they fail (e.g., privacy, realistic micro-expressions, no subscription lock-in).
